All About Me

My name is Backy S. Ofo-ob, and I am 20 years old, currently living in the cool and scenic city of Baguio. I am a student at the University of Baguio, majoring in Information Technology. The fast-paced world of tech has always intrigued me, and I am passionate about learning the ins and outs of how technology shapes our everyday lives. My goal is to apply the skills I am learning to make a meaningful impact in the future.

When I’m not busy with my studies, I like to unwind by indulging in two of my favorite things: coffee and my cellphone. I’m a true coffee lover, and there’s nothing better than sipping on a warm cup while taking a break or brainstorming ideas. As for my free time, I often spend it using my cellphone, whether browsing social media, chatting with friends, or catching up on the latest tech trends. I enjoy staying connected and informed, especially about the latest in the tech world.

Living in Baguio City has its perks, with the cool weather and stunning views being just some of the many advantages. It’s a great place to balance the demands of college life with some relaxation. Whenever I get the chance, I love walking around the parks or enjoying the famous Baguio strawberries. The city also has a rich cultural history, which makes every day here feel unique. Baguio is the perfect place to grow, both personally and academically.
